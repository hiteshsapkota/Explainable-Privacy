Welcome to the user study in Explainable Privacy

Please read the instructions carefully before proceeding to the survey.

This study will have following two phases:

Training

In training phase, total twenty images will be shown and will be asked whether you want to share or not. Sharing decision should be made based on the assumption that you are one of the people in the picture. Also, if you do not wish to answer, simply skip the image and go to next. You cannot start the evaluation phase without the completion of a training phase

Evaluation

In the evaluation phase, an image will be shown along with suggestion regarding sharing decision. Suggestion will have two parts: Decision and Explanation. A decision will tell you whether you want to share or not whereas, an explanation will tell you why you want to make the corresponding sharing decision. You need to decide whether you agree with either or both of the decision and explanation. In case of disagree you are required to provide the disagreement type (Decision, Recommendation, Both). Also, you need to provide the sensitivity score for the listed attributes with 5 as a strongly sensitive and 1 as a least sensitive to the sharing decision.

A User Study on Explainable Privacy

Welcome! In this HIT, you will participate in a user study on privacy recommendations and explanations. In short, you will be shown a series of pictures and asked your preferences about sharing those pictures on the Internet. More details on the study will be provided in the next page.

We estimate that participating in the entire study will take about 20 minutes. Once you complete the study, a completion code will be generated. You can use this code to submit your HIT on the MTurk website.

Your must first register on this website to participate in the study. Once registered, use your username and password to login to the website.

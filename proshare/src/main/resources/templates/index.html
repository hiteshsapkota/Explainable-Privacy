    Survey Instruction

    Training

    In this survey you need to complete two steps: training followed by the evaluation. In either of steps, in each batch twenty images will be shown to you. In the case of training phase, series of images will be shown and asked to provide the sharing decision. Sharing decision would be either share or not based on the assumption that you are one of the persons in the images. If you do not wish to provide the decision, you can skip using Next button. Also, you if you want to change the decision, you can use Prev button.

    Click here to start training

    Evaluation

    You can start the evaluation step after the completion of at least one training phase. In the evaluation phase, series of images will be shown along with the corresponding suggested sharing decision along with the explanation. The sharing decision tells whether you want to share the image or not whereas, the explanation explains why you want to make the specified sharing decision. You can either agree or diagree with the generated decision, explanation or both. If you disagree then you need to specify your disagreement type as one of the decision, explanation or both. Also, in case of disagreement, you have to specify the sensitivity score for each of the attributes shown with 5 as strongly sensitive and 1 as least sensitive.
